# Style Catalogue

Use this to pick a style for the user, based on (a) the **content / purpose** of their board and
(b) the **vibe** they want. If the user named a style or a clear preference, honour it. Otherwise
infer from the content and propose one. After generating, always tell the user they can switch to
any other style.

**Formality key:** **Low** = playful / casual, **Medium** = confident / modern, **High** =
restrained / refined / institutional.

## How to choose

1. **Content first.** Match the board's purpose to a row in **Good for**. A formal research brief
   leans High formality (Reading Room, Linen Cut, Cobalt Glaze); a playful kickoff leans Low
   (BlockFrame, Confetti Wedge, Crayon Stack); a general explainer or process sits comfortably in
   Medium (Riso Brut, Raw Grid, Neo-Grid Bold).
2. **Vibe second.** Narrow by the user's stated mood or colour preference.
3. **When unsure, default to a versatile Medium** style (Riso Brut is the safe, designed default).
4. Always offer to switch afterwards. Swapping style means re-rendering the same content in a
   different template's palette.
